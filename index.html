    Our Dishes

    We serve different Dishes . You can choose from below

    • Pancake Dishes

      Proving your topping doesn't have to go...er... on top, these sophisticated fritters make a great brunch or even a starter if you're celebrating in the evening.

    • Blueberry Pancakes

      Tall, fluffy pancakes are delicious served with butter, syrup, and whipped cream. These tasty pancakes taste like cake. Just go by your instinct and enjoy the baking. Yummy!"

    • Fruits Dishes

      When fruit is not in season, head to the freezer aisle! Frozen fruit is just as healthy as fresh fruit, and is often way cheaper. I love using frozen fruit in my parfaits because they thaw slowly and it helps them last for longer.

    • Chocolate Pancake

      Everyone loves pancakes but chocolate pancakes are even nicer! Team them with chocolate sauce, strawberries and vanilla ice cream for a delicious dessert.
